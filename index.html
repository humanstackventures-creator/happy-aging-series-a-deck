APPENDIX
Unit economics improving every quarter
Unit economics improving as we rationalized the SKU portfolio, shifted to capsules, and refined acquisition strategy. Latest cohorts reaching M3 payback at contribution margins, a first since launch, with CAC down 35% from peak.
Early cohorts acquired at $47–$77 CAC paid back within their first order cycle and reached 4x+ LTV:CAC by M18. During 2025, we scaled aggressively, testing 25 SKUs and expanding channels, pushing blended CAC to $140. We've since rationalized to capsules, tightened creative, and cut CAC 35% from peak. Jan–Feb '26 cohorts are already hitting M3 contribution payback at $117–$129 CAC, the strongest early economics since launch. Mar–May '26 cohorts are tracking ahead on early retention with CAC continuing to compress ($118–$136), signaling further improvement as they mature.
